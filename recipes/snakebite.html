The Snakebite Cocktail

Description

The Snakebite cocktail is an alcoholic drink historically made of lager and hard cider. This version uses Guiness instead for an American twist on the UK original

Ingredients

  • 1 part Guiness Irish Stout
  • 1 part Sommersby Hard Apple Cider

Steps

  1. Pour half a glass of Sommersby Hard Apple Cider
  2. Use a barspoon to slowly pour the Guiness until the glass is full
